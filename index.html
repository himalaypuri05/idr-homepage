About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute focused on developing specialists in digital, cyber and technology risk roles across regulated and high-consequence sectors.

Working in collaboration with academic partners including UK universities, IDR connects academic insight with real-world industry challenges, allowing students and professionals to work on practical risk management projects.

Our Model

Academy

Professional training programs and bootcamps designed to prepare students and practitioners for careers in digital and cyber risk.

Innovation & Incubation

Developing new intellectual property including digital risk models, AI governance frameworks and resilience tools.

Advisory Services

Helping organisations implement global standards such as NIST, ISO 27001 and NIS2 across complex technology environments.
